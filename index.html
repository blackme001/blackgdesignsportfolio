What industries do you specialise in?
↓
I work primarily in real estate, construction, health & wellness, and e-commerce — four verticals where strong digital products drive the highest measurable ROI. That said, I'm selective, not exclusive; if your project is compelling and you're serious about results, let's talk.
How long does a typical project take?
↓
Timelines depend heavily on scope. A focused MVP typically ships in 6–10 weeks. Full-scale product builds with design systems and backend infrastructure range from 3–6 months. I'll give you an honest estimate after our discovery call — no inflated timelines to pad hours.
Do you take on ongoing retainer work?
↓
Yes. Many clients engage me post-launch for continuous product iteration, performance monitoring, and feature development. Retainer engagements start at a minimum of 20 hours/month and include a dedicated Slack channel for rapid communication.
What's your rate structure?
↓
I work on project-based pricing for defined scopes — not hourly billing. This aligns my incentives with your outcomes. Project minimums start at $5,000. For enterprise engagements and retainers, custom packages are available. Schedule a call to discuss.
