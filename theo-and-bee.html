The Challenge

In the heavy industrial and construction logistics sector, lead generation is traditionally heavily manual. For specialized equipment hire firms like Theo & Bee Global Resources—which operates premium crawler mini cranes used in skyscraper mounts, roof decking, and solar installations—securing corporate tenders was limited by poor digital visibility. Potential construction foremen and building developers were unable to view the exact specifications of the mini cranes, hire packages, or submit fast digital requests for tenders online.

The goal was to design a professional, high-authority web platform that showcases their physical crane fleet, outlines structural lifting capacities, highlights successful commercial case studies, and converts visitors into verified quoting opportunities.

The Engineered Solution

I designed and engineered the Theo & Bee Global Resources corporate platform inside a performance-optimized Next.js and Tailwind CSS setup. The platform is styled with high-contrast structural accents (bold orange and dark slate), reflecting the high-end machinery and industrial authority of the firm.

To reduce quoting bottlenecks, I developed an interactive corporate inquiry flow where builders can specify crane rental days, structural heights, and lift weights to submit clean requests. The platform presents a curated equipment gallery with accurate specifications (crawler widths, fly-jib extensions, skyscraper mount options), helping structural estimators quickly confirm crane fits and complete tender forms.

Key Engineered Features

  • Sleek, high-authority dark corporate aesthetic tailored for commercial builders.
  • Interactive mini crane catalog detailing boom extensions, load charts, and fly-jibs.
  • Optimized Quote & Tender CTA sequence connected directly to lead storage nodes.
  • Performance-tuned architecture yielding a 98 PageSpeed score for structural engineers in the field.
