The Challenge

For over 30 years, Black Estates has been crafting extraordinary custom homes across Central Ohio. However, their digital presence did not match the premium, award-winning craftsmanship of their physical builds. The objective was to design a state-of-the-art web platform that immediately establishes them as the region's premier luxury builder.

The platform needed to showcase an extensive library of 500+ customizable floor plans, highlight their immersive 10,000 sq. ft. Home Front Design Studio, and effortlessly convert high-net-worth clients into direct design consultations.

The Engineered Solution

I architected the Black Estates platform with a focus on immersive visuals and frictionless user journeys. Using modern tech stacks and AI automation, I developed a lightning-fast, highly responsive portal that acts as a digital showroom for their luxury properties.

The site features dynamic photo and video galleries, detailed community and location guides, and a streamlined consultation booking flow. By prioritizing performance and elegant, dark-mode aesthetics, the platform delivers a premium experience that builds immediate trust and significantly increases high-value tender inquiries.

Key Engineered Features

  • High-fidelity, performant galleries showcasing luxury custom homes and 500+ floor plans.
  • Interactive location maps detailing active building communities across Central Ohio.
  • Optimized dynamic image delivery utilizing next-gen formats for superfast mobile load times.
  • Frictionless contact funnels directly routing to design specialists and consultation teams.
